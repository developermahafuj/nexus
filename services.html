Choosing the right starting point

Start with the situation, not the technical label.

Nexus routes each inquiry according to urgency, affected asset, business impact and the evidence already available.

Active incident

Something is compromised right now.

Use emergency intake for hacked websites, account takeover or active malicious behavior.

Preventive security

You need to find weaknesses before an attacker does.

Begin with VAPT or an exposure audit depending on the asset and required depth.

Exposure and reputation

Your public digital footprint is creating risk.

OSINT, content-removal and reputation work can clarify the source and possible response.

Secure growth

You want automation without careless data handling.

Secure AI automation focuses on useful workflows, integrations and access boundaries.
